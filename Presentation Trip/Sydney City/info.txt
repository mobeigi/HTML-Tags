Sydney - famous for its wonderful harbour, the Opera House and the Harbor Bridge. One of the most beautiful and exciting great cities of the world. It is a cosmopolitan metropolis where art, science,
commerce and sport all flourish. Students can enjoy the outdoor life, walks and pristine beaches in easy reach of the city centre.

It has the deepest natural harbour in the world with 504,00 mega litres of water.

Construction on the Sydney Harbour Bridge started in 1924. It took 1,500 men, eight years, 53,000 tonnes of steel and six million hand driven rivets to complete the world’s biggest Meccano Set.
